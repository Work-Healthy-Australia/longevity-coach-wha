# Phase 2 — Intelligence: Risk Scoring, Biological Age & Personalised Report

**Status:** In progress  
**Goal:** A member who has completed their questionnaire automatically receives a biological age score, a five-domain health risk breakdown, and a personalised supplement protocol delivered as a branded downloadable report.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 2.1 — Biological Age Assessment

Members need a single, meaningful number that summarises their current health status relative to their age.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 2.1.1 | As a member who has completed the health questionnaire, I automatically receive a biological age score based on my responses. |
| 2.1.2 | As a member, I can see how my biological age compares to my chronological age in a clear, visual format. |
| 2.1.3 | As a member, I understand what factors contributed most to my biological age score. |
| 2.1.4 | As a member, I can see how my score is expected to change if I adopt the recommended actions. |

---

## Epic 2.2 — Five-Domain Risk Breakdown

Members need to understand their health risks across multiple dimensions, not just as a single number.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 2.2.1 | As a member, I can see my risk level across five health domains: cardiovascular, metabolic, neurological, oncological, and musculoskeletal. |
| 2.2.2 | As a member, each domain is explained in plain language so I understand what it means for my daily life. |
| 2.2.3 | As a member, I can see which domains have the greatest opportunity for improvement. |
| 2.2.4 | As a member, my domain risk scores are automatically updated when I provide new health data. |

---

## Epic 2.3 — Personalised Supplement Protocol

Members need a concrete, evidence-backed starting point for improving their health — not just a diagnosis.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 2.3.1 | As a member with a completed risk assessment, I receive a personalised 30-day supplement protocol matched to my specific risk profile. |
| 2.3.2 | As a member, each supplement recommendation is explained with a plain-language rationale linked to my specific health drivers. |
| 2.3.3 | As a member, my supplement protocol is updated when my risk scores change. |
| 2.3.4 | As a member, I can ask the AI Supplement Advisor questions about any recommendation in my protocol. |

---

## Epic 2.4 — Branded Health Report (PDF)

Members need a professional document they can share with a doctor, show a partner, or keep as a personal record.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 2.4.1 | As a member, I can download a branded PDF report that includes my biological age, domain risk scores, top health drivers, and supplement protocol. |
| 2.4.2 | As a member, my report is personalised with my name, assessment date, and results. |
| 2.4.3 | As a member, the report is written in plain English with no unexplained medical abbreviations. |
| 2.4.4 | As a member, I can generate an updated report at any time to reflect my most recent data. |

---

## Epic 2.5 — Personal Report Screen

Members need an in-app view of their full results — accessible at any time without downloading a PDF.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 2.5.1 | As a member, I can view my full health report within the app, including bio-age, risk domains, and supplement protocol. |
| 2.5.2 | As a member, my report screen shows the date my data was last updated. |
| 2.5.3 | As a member, I can navigate from my report directly into the relevant coaching or action area for each risk domain. |
