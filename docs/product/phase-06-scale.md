# Phase 6 — Scale: Corporate Wellness & Marketplace

**Status:** Planned  
**Goal:** Open the platform to enterprise buyers and expand member value through supplement purchasing and a corporate wellness offering.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 6.1 — Corporate Wellness Programme

HR and wellness managers need a way to deploy Longevity Coach across their workforce and measure the outcomes.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 6.1.1 | As a corporate wellness manager, I can create a company account and invite employees to join the platform. |
| 6.1.2 | As a corporate wellness manager, I can see aggregate health and engagement metrics for my workforce without seeing any individual's personal data. |
| 6.1.3 | As a corporate wellness manager, I can run company-wide challenges and track participation and completion rates. |
| 6.1.4 | As an employee on a corporate plan, I receive the same personalised health assessment, report, and coaching features as an individual member. |
| 6.1.5 | As a corporate buyer, I can access consolidated invoicing and account reporting for our organisation. |

---

## Epic 6.2 — Admin CRM

The Longevity Coach team needs operational visibility to support members, manage accounts, and monitor the health of the business.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 6.2.1 | As an administrator, I can view all registered members with their subscription status and assessment completion stage. |
| 6.2.2 | As an administrator, I can search for a specific member and view the details of their account. |
| 6.2.3 | As an administrator, I can see key business metrics — total members, active subscribers, assessment completion rate, and revenue — on a single dashboard. |
| 6.2.4 | As an administrator, I can manually update a member's subscription status when required to resolve an issue. |

---

## Epic 6.3 — Supplement Marketplace

Members who want to act on their protocol should be able to purchase recommended supplements without leaving the platform.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 6.3.1 | As a member, I can purchase supplements directly from within my personalised protocol. |
| 6.3.2 | As a member, I can see the evidence rating and sourcing information for each supplement before purchasing. |
| 6.3.3 | As a member, my purchase history is tracked so the platform can monitor how closely I am following my protocol. |
| 6.3.4 | As a member, I can set up automatic replenishment for supplements on a recurring cycle so I never run out. |
