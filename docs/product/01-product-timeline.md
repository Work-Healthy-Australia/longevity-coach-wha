## Phase 1 — Foundation: Identity, Onboarding & Payments

**Status:** Complete as of 2026-04-24

**Goal:** A stranger can discover the product, understand the offer, create an account, complete a health questionnaire, and pay for a subscription.

**Key outcomes delivered:**
- Public marketing site with Science, Team, Stories, and Sample Report pages
- Full account creation, email verification, and password reset flows
- Six-step health questionnaire with save-and-resume
- Stripe subscription checkout and billing management
- Signed-in dashboard showing assessment status and subscription state
- Automated welcome email on account verification

**Epics:** [Phase 1 detail →](./phase-01-foundation.md)

## Phase 2 — Intelligence: Risk Scoring, Biological Age & Personalised Report

**Status:** In progress

**Goal:** A member who has completed their questionnaire automatically receives a biological age score, a five-domain health risk breakdown, and a personalised supplement protocol delivered as a branded downloadable report.

**Key outcomes to deliver:**
- Biological age score calculated from questionnaire responses
- Five-domain risk breakdown: cardiovascular, metabolic, neurological, oncological, musculoskeletal
- Personalised 30-day supplement protocol matched to the member's risk profile
- Branded, downloadable PDF health report
- In-app report screen accessible at any time

**Epics:** [Phase 2 detail →](./phase-02-intelligence.md)

---

## Phase 3 — Engagement: Daily Habits, AI Coaching & Nutrition

**Status:** Planned

**Goal:** Members return daily. The platform becomes a routine companion — tracking habits, delivering coaching, and helping members make better food choices.

**Key outcomes to deliver:**
- Daily check-in capturing weight, mood, energy, and sleep
- Personalised daily goals with habit streak tracking
- AI coaching suite: nutrition coach, supplement advisor, fitness coach, general health coach
- Weekly meal planning with auto-generated shopping lists and meal logging
- Health journal and personalised insights feed

**Epics:** [Phase 3 detail →](./phase-03-engagement.md)

---

## Phase 4 — Clinical Depth: Biomarker Uploads & Advanced Tracking

**Status:** Planned

**Goal:** Members who want to go deeper can upload lab results, connect wearables, and track biomarkers over time — unlocking more precise risk scoring and clinical-grade insights.

**Key outcomes to deliver:**
- Upload and automatic parsing of blood tests, imaging, genetic, microbiome, and hormone reports
- Longitudinal biomarker charts with trend analysis and out-of-range alerts
- Wearable device connections (Oura, Apple Watch, Garmin) and manual metric entry
- Interactive risk simulator showing the projected impact of lifestyle changes on biological age

**Epics:** [Phase 4 detail →](./phase-04-clinical-depth.md)

## Phase 5 — Care Network: Clinical Portal & Community

**Status:** Planned

**Goal:** Extend the platform beyond the individual member — enabling practitioners to manage patient longevity care and members to participate in a wider community.

**Key outcomes to deliver:**
- Clinician portal with patient overview, clinical notes, and biomarker alerts
- Care team invitation and permission management for members
- In-platform appointment booking with care team members
- Community challenges, leaderboard, achievement badges, and member feed

**Epics:** [Phase 5 detail →](./phase-05-care-network.md)

## Phase 6 — Scale: Corporate Wellness & Marketplace

**Status:** Planned

**Goal:** Open the platform to enterprise buyers and expand member value through supplement purchasing.

**Key outcomes to deliver:**
- Corporate account management with employee invitations, team challenges, and aggregate reporting
- Admin CRM for the Longevity Coach team to manage members and monitor the business

## Platform-Wide Definition of Done

The platform is considered complete when a member can:

1. Discover the product online and understand the offer before signing up
2. Create an account, complete a health questionnaire, and pay for a subscription
3. Receive a biological age score and five-domain risk breakdown
4. Download a branded health report and personalised supplement protocol
5. Return daily to track habits, chat with coaches, and log meals
6. Upload lab results and connect wearables to deepen their risk picture
7. Share their data with a clinician and book appointments within the platform
8. Participate in community challenges and earn recognition for their progress
9. Purchase recommended supplements directly through the platform
10. Access the platform through their employer as part of a corporate wellness programme
