# Phase 4 — Clinical Depth: Biomarker Uploads & Advanced Tracking

**Status:** Planned  
**Goal:** Members who want to go deeper can upload lab results, connect wearables, and track biomarkers over time — unlocking more precise risk scoring and clinical-grade insights.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 4.1 — Lab Results & File Uploads

Members need a way to bring their existing medical data into the platform so their risk assessment reflects the full picture.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 4.1.1 | As a member, I can upload blood test results, imaging reports, genetic tests, microbiome panels, and hormone panels. |
| 4.1.2 | As a member, uploaded documents are automatically parsed and the relevant values are extracted and added to my health profile. |
| 4.1.3 | As a member, I can see which biomarkers have been detected in my uploads and which are still missing. |
| 4.1.4 | As a member, my risk scores are automatically refined when new lab data is added. |

---

## Epic 4.2 — Biomarker Trend Tracking

Members need to see whether their health is improving over time, not just where they stand today.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 4.2.1 | As a member, I can view a chart of any health marker over time — including cholesterol, blood glucose, and inflammatory markers. |
| 4.2.2 | As a member, I can see at a glance whether a marker is moving towards or away from the healthy range. |
| 4.2.3 | As a member, the platform alerts me when a health marker moves outside a safe reference range. |
| 4.2.4 | As a member, I can see how lifestyle changes I have made correlate with improvements in my health markers. |

---

## Epic 4.3 — Wearable & Device Integration

Members who use health tracking devices should be able to bring that data into their health profile without manual effort.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 4.3.1 | As a member, I can connect a wearable device and have my sleep, activity, and heart rate data flow into my profile automatically. |
| 4.3.2 | As a member, my daily goals reflect real data from my connected device rather than generic defaults. |
| 4.3.3 | As a member, I can manually enter health readings (blood pressure, weight, glucose) when I do not have a connected device. |
| 4.3.4 | As a member, data from my wearable contributes to my biological age score over time. |

---

## Epic 4.4 — Risk Simulator

Members need to see the projected impact of lifestyle choices before they commit to changing them.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 4.4.1 | As a member, I can adjust lifestyle variables — sleep, exercise frequency, diet quality, supplement adherence — and see how my projected biological age changes. |
| 4.4.2 | As a member, the simulator ranks interventions by their relative impact so I know where to focus first. |
| 4.4.3 | As a member, I can save a simulated scenario as a personal health goal and track my real progress against it over time. |
