# Longevity Coach — Product Overview

**Document type:** Executive product brief  
**Audience:** Product owners, investors, clinical advisors  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-27

---

## What Is Longevity Coach?

Longevity Coach is a personal health optimisation platform that transforms how people understand, manage, and extend their healthspan. It combines a comprehensive health assessment with AI-powered coaching, personalised supplement protocols, and biological age tracking to give members a clear, evidence-based picture of where they are today — and a concrete plan for where they want to be.

---

## Who It Serves

The platform is designed for three distinct audiences, each with a specific value proposition.

**Individual members** want data-driven, personalised guidance on living longer and better. They receive a biological age score, a five-domain health risk breakdown, and a personalised action plan they can act on immediately.

**Corporate wellness buyers** want to offer meaningful health benefits at scale and demonstrate measurable outcomes to their workforce. They receive aggregate reporting, team-based challenges, and consolidated account management.

**Clinical practitioners** want a structured tool for managing patient longevity care. They receive a patient overview screen with risk summaries, clinical notes, and alerts when patient data changes significantly.

---

## Core Value Proposition

| For | The platform delivers |
|---|---|
| Individual member | A single number — their biological age — and a personalised, actionable plan to improve it |
| Corporate wellness buyer | Measurable employee wellness outcomes with aggregate reporting and ROI visibility |
| Clinical practitioner | A structured view of patient health data with AI-assisted risk summaries and clinical tools |

---

## What Makes It Different

Most health platforms show members data and leave them to figure out what to do with it. Longevity Coach closes that gap by pairing every assessment with a concrete protocol — a specific supplement plan, targeted daily habits, and AI coaches that understand the member's personal health context.

The platform is built around four core capabilities:

1. **Assessment** — A multi-domain health questionnaire and risk scoring engine that produces a biological age and a breakdown of the member's five health risk domains.
2. **Protocol** — A personalised 30-day supplement plan, daily habit targets, and a meal plan — all derived from the member's specific risk profile.
3. **Coaching** — A suite of AI coaches (nutrition, supplements, fitness, general health) that understand the member's history and give contextual, personalised guidance.
4. **Tracking** — Longitudinal monitoring of biomarkers, daily habits, and biological age over time so members can see whether their choices are working.

---

## Business Model

- **Individual subscriptions** — monthly and annual plans giving access to the full assessment, report, coaching, and tracking suite
- **Corporate wellness licences** — enterprise accounts for HR and wellness teams with aggregate dashboards and team challenges
- **Supplement marketplace** — in-platform purchasing of protocol-recommended supplements with auto-replenishment
- **Clinical tier** — practitioner access with patient management tools, clinical notes, and care team features
