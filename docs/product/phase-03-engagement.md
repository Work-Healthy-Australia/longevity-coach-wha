# Phase 3 — Engagement: Daily Habits, AI Coaching & Nutrition

**Status:** Planned  
**Goal:** Members return daily. The platform becomes a routine companion — tracking habits, delivering coaching, and helping members make better food choices.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 3.1 — Daily Check-In

Members need a quick, lightweight daily ritual that keeps them connected to their health goals.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 3.1.1 | As a member, I can complete a daily check-in that captures my weight, mood, energy level, and sleep quality in under two minutes. |
| 3.1.2 | As a member, I can see a visual history of my check-in data over time. |
| 3.1.3 | As a member, I receive a gentle daily reminder to complete my check-in. |
| 3.1.4 | As a member, the platform surfaces insights from my check-in patterns ("Your energy is consistently low on Mondays — here is why that might be"). |

---

## Epic 3.2 — Daily Goals & Habit Tracking

Members need clear, actionable daily targets that turn their health plan into concrete behaviours.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 3.2.1 | As a member, I am given a set of personalised daily goals (steps, water intake, sleep target, meditation) based on my risk profile. |
| 3.2.2 | As a member, I can mark goals as complete during the day and see my completion rate at a glance. |
| 3.2.3 | As a member, I can see my habit streaks so I have a reason to return each day. |
| 3.2.4 | As a member, my daily goals are adjusted over time as my health data evolves. |

---

## Epic 3.3 — AI Health Coaches

Members need on-demand, personalised guidance — not generic advice.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 3.3.1 | As a member, I can chat with an AI health coach that understands my personal health history and goals. |
| 3.3.2 | As a member, I can ask the nutrition coach for meal ideas, recipe suggestions, and food choices that support my bloodwork results. |
| 3.3.3 | As a member, I can ask the supplement advisor for clarity on any recommendation in my protocol. |
| 3.3.4 | As a member, I can ask the fitness coach to suggest an exercise session based on my current energy level and goals. |
| 3.3.5 | As a member, conversations with coaches are saved so I do not have to repeat context each time. |

---

## Epic 3.4 — Meal Planning & Nutrition

Members need practical nutrition guidance that connects their health goals to what they actually eat.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 3.4.1 | As a member, I can view a personalised weekly meal plan based on my health goals and dietary preferences. |
| 3.4.2 | As a member, I can generate an automatic shopping list from my meal plan. |
| 3.4.3 | As a member, I can log a meal and see how it contributes to my daily nutrition targets. |
| 3.4.4 | As a member, the nutrition coach can discover and recommend trending healthy recipes from across the web matched to my dietary targets. |
| 3.4.5 | As a member, my meal plan is updated when my bloodwork or health goals change. |

---

## Epic 3.5 — Health Journal & Insights Feed

Members need a space to reflect on their health journey and receive timely, relevant insights.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 3.5.1 | As a member, I can write private journal entries about my health experiences and feelings. |
| 3.5.2 | As a member, the platform surfaces insights from patterns in my journal entries. |
| 3.5.3 | As a member, I receive a personalised feed of health news, tips, and evidence-based recommendations relevant to my risk profile. |
