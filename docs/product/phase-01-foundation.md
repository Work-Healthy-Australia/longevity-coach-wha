# Phase 1 — Foundation: Identity, Onboarding & Payments

**Status:** Complete as of 2026-04-24  
**Goal:** A stranger can discover the product, understand the offer, create an account, complete a health questionnaire, and pay for a subscription.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 1.1 — Public Marketing Presence

Members need to understand what Longevity Coach is and why it matters before they commit.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 1.1.1 | As a visitor, I can read about the science behind biological age testing so I understand why this approach is credible. |
| 1.1.2 | As a visitor, I can meet the founding team so I feel confident in the organisation behind the product. |
| 1.1.3 | As a visitor, I can read real member stories so I can see results that other people like me have achieved. |
| 1.1.4 | As a visitor, I can view a worked example of a full health report so I know exactly what I am buying before I pay. |
| 1.1.5 | As a visitor, I can navigate between Science, Team, and Stories sections without losing context of the main offer. |

---

## Epic 1.2 — Account Creation & Verification

Members need a secure, frictionless way to create and verify their account.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 1.2.1 | As a new visitor, I can sign up with my email address and a password. |
| 1.2.2 | As a new member, I receive a verification email and my account is activated when I confirm my address. |
| 1.2.3 | As a returning member, I can sign in and be taken directly to my personal dashboard. |
| 1.2.4 | As a member who has forgotten their password, I can request a reset link by email and set a new password. |
| 1.2.5 | As a verified member, I receive a personalised welcome email confirming my account is active and explaining my next steps. |

---

## Epic 1.3 — Health Questionnaire

Members need a structured way to share their health background so the platform can build a personalised baseline.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 1.3.1 | As a new member, I am guided through a step-by-step health questionnaire covering personal basics, medical history, family history, lifestyle, health goals, and consent. |
| 1.3.2 | As a member mid-questionnaire, my progress is saved automatically so I can return later without losing my answers. |
| 1.3.3 | As a member, I understand why each section of the questionnaire is needed through clear, plain-English descriptions. |
| 1.3.4 | As a member who has completed the questionnaire, I am taken to my dashboard and shown what happens next. |

---

## Epic 1.4 — Subscription & Payment

Members need a simple, trustworthy way to start a paid plan.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 1.4.1 | As a member ready to subscribe, I can choose between a monthly and an annual plan. |
| 1.4.2 | As a member, I am taken to a secure payment page and can complete checkout without leaving the product. |
| 1.4.3 | As a member, my subscription status is immediately reflected in my account after successful payment. |
| 1.4.4 | As a subscriber, my billing status is kept up to date automatically when payments succeed, fail, or are cancelled. |

---

## Epic 1.5 — Personal Dashboard (Foundation)

Members need a home screen that shows where they are today and what to do next.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 1.5.1 | As a signed-in member, I can see my assessment status — whether I have not yet started, am in progress, or have completed it. |
| 1.5.2 | As a signed-in member, I can see my current subscription status at a glance. |
| 1.5.3 | As a member who has completed their assessment, I am shown a placeholder for my risk scores while the system processes my results. |
| 1.5.4 | As a member, the primary call-to-action on my dashboard always reflects the most logical next step in my journey. |
