# Phase 5 — Care Network: Clinical Portal & Community

**Status:** Planned  
**Goal:** Extend the platform beyond the individual member — enabling practitioners to manage patient longevity care and members to participate in a wider community.

← [Back to Product Timeline](./01-product-timeline.md)

---

## Epic 5.1 — Clinician Portal

Practitioners need a structured view of their patients' health data with the context to act on it efficiently.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 5.1.1 | As a clinician, I can access a secure portal showing all patients who have granted me access to their health data. |
| 5.1.2 | As a clinician, I can review a patient's biological age, domain risk scores, biomarker trends, and supplement protocol from a single screen. |
| 5.1.3 | As a clinician, I can read and write structured clinical notes that are attached to a patient's record. |
| 5.1.4 | As a clinician, I can review a patient's daily check-in history to identify patterns between appointment sessions. |
| 5.1.5 | As a clinician, I receive an alert when a patient's health marker or check-in data moves outside a safe range. |

---

## Epic 5.2 — Care Team Sharing

Members should be able to involve their existing healthcare providers in their longevity journey.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 5.2.1 | As a member, I can invite a clinician or health coach to view my health data by sending them a secure invitation. |
| 5.2.2 | As a member, I can control exactly which parts of my data a care team member can access. |
| 5.2.3 | As a member, I can revoke access for any care team member at any time. |
| 5.2.4 | As a member, I can book an appointment with a member of my care team directly within the platform. |

---

## Epic 5.3 — Community & Challenges

Members are more likely to sustain healthy behaviours when connected to a community with shared goals.

| # | User Story |
|---|---|
| 5.3.1 | As a member, I can join a community challenge — such as a 30-day steps or sleep improvement challenge — and track my progress on a shared leaderboard. |
| 5.3.2 | As a member, I can view a community feed of achievements and milestones from other members. |
| 5.3.3 | As a member, I earn badges and recognition for completing personal health milestones. |
| 5.3.4 | As a member who values privacy, I can keep all my personal data private and still access the full platform. |
